# Privacy Policy - Stoflow Browser Extension

**Last Updated:** January 19, 2026

## Overview

Stoflow ("we", "our", or "the extension") is a browser extension that helps users manage their e-commerce listings across multiple marketplaces, starting with Vinted.

This Privacy Policy explains how the Stoflow browser extension collects, uses, and protects your information.

---

## Data Collection

### What We Collect

The Stoflow extension collects and processes the following data:

1. **Vinted Account Information**
   - User ID and username (for identifying your Vinted account)
   - Product listings data (titles, descriptions, prices, photos)
   - Transaction history (for sync purposes)

2. **Extension Usage Data**
   - Actions performed through the extension
   - Error logs (for debugging, no personal data included)

3. **Authentication Tokens**
   - Session tokens for Vinted API access (stored locally in browser)

### What We Do NOT Collect

- Passwords (we never see or store your Vinted password)
- Payment information (credit cards, bank accounts)
- Personal identification documents
- Browsing history outside of Vinted
- Location data
- Device identifiers

---

## Data Usage

Your data is used exclusively to:

1. **Sync your listings** between Stoflow and Vinted
2. **Display your products** in the Stoflow dashboard
3. **Execute actions** you request (publish, update, delete listings)
4. **Improve the extension** through anonymized error reporting

---

## Data Storage

### Local Storage

- Session tokens and preferences are stored locally in your browser using Chrome's secure storage API (`chrome.storage.local`)
- This data never leaves your browser unless you explicitly perform an action

### Server Storage

- Product data is synced with Stoflow servers (api.stoflow.io) to enable cross-device access
- All data is encrypted in transit (HTTPS/TLS)
- Data is stored in EU-based servers compliant with GDPR

---

## Data Sharing

We do NOT:
- Sell your data to third parties
- Share your data with advertisers
- Use your data for profiling or targeted advertising

We MAY share data:
- With Vinted API (to perform the actions you request)
- With law enforcement (if legally required)

---

## Your Rights

You have the right to:

1. **Access** your data stored by Stoflow
2. **Delete** your data at any time
3. **Export** your data in a portable format
4. **Opt-out** of data collection by uninstalling the extension

To exercise these rights, contact us at: privacy@stoflow.io

---

## Security

We implement industry-standard security measures:

- All communications use HTTPS/TLS encryption
- Authentication tokens are stored in browser's encrypted storage
- No sensitive data is logged or transmitted to our servers
- Regular security audits and updates

---

## Third-Party Services

The extension interacts with:

1. **Vinted API** (www.vinted.fr, www.vinted.com)
   - Subject to [Vinted's Privacy Policy](https://www.vinted.fr/privacy-policy)

2. **Stoflow Backend** (api.stoflow.io)
   - Subject to this Privacy Policy
